Overview

The headline read on AI-generated-code search demand.

AI-generated code is creating real compliance, security and quality pressure for development teams, and search demand around it is splitting into distinct topics moving at different speeds. Security is by far the largest area, but it has steadied and is more competitive. Governance is the standout emerging opportunity. Lower volume today, but the only accelerating trend. Slopsquatting has settled into a durable niche with strong PR/link potential. Quality and compliance are sizable but maturing.
